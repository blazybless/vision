    February 20, 2017

    Great dress!! I had ordered this dress after I had purchased another dress online that came in the wrong size :( - not from Amazon though. With my wedding just two (2) weeks away, I had already gone to a bridal shop with the intention to rent a gown and was looking at something else on Amazon and this dress came up in my feed. Awesome reviews!!!! Lots of pictures!!! and everyone says it shipped quickly. I too received the dress quickly, I purchased it on the Thursday and it came on the Saturday. I couldn't have asked for better service.

    A good seamstress is really all you need to personalize this dress and I made some alterations to the dress. I am normally a size 12 but ordered a 16 just to make sure I didn't get a size that was too small. I ended up adjusting the sides a bit, as well as the front pelvic area. I also cut the front of the dress because it was too long and I would have tripped over it, had I worn it as purchased. I also added snap buttons to the back flap so that it would close properly and so that I could lace the back of the dress properly.

    The lace on the dress is beautiful and does not look cheap at all.

    The string lacing at the back of the dress is pretty but does not offer much support, even if you are even slightly heavy on top.

    Ensure that the sides fit you well, or else the dress will start slipping, and you dont want that in the middle of your ceremony.

    Also ensure that you wear a good push up bra and get some strong two-sided fabric tape.
